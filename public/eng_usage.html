A Guide to Sixtus Cloud Scanner

There are two main groups of users of the Sixtus Scanner: employees tasked with data minimisation or enforcing company policy and responsible(s).

The guide shall refers to the second group as "responsibles", which is a purposefully vague term for those given/hired with the responsibility of GDPR compliance.

These two purposes are widely different and shall have each their own section.

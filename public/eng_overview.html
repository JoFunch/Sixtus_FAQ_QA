Overview-page

This is your personal overview-site. It will show you your MS-account's scanned data according to data classification and time.

You can click around the charts and table to narrow the subsection of data you want to examine.

The front page consists of the Top-bar statistical overview of the Tenant, two pie-charts and a diachronic table of data.



The statistical top-bar





The top bar has 8 points divided into a total number and a count. The total numbers have the colour blue while the red colour shows count.

  1. Emails ("Scanned" and "GDPR") represent the total emails scanned in the Tenant and the count of emails in which there has been found any number of GDPR-related cases (If 1 email has 2 cases of GDPR, 1 in the body and two in an attachment, there is 1 count of "Email GDPR" and 1 "Scanned" email). (What if the email has 0 count but 1 in attachments?)

  2. Attachments ("Scanned" and "GDPR") are similar to "Emails", only that these two numbers only concern themselves with the attachments of emails (and not OneDrive or SharePoint)

  3. Files ("Scanned" and "GDPR") are exactly those of OneDrive and SharePoint collected into one.

  4. Accounts is a stand-alone count and represents the number of Tenant Accounts scanned.

  5. GDPR Cases are the count of the total amount of Cases /observations of GDPR in the entire Tenant. These include all Cases found in Email, OneDrive, SharePoint, and Attachments. 1 GDPR Case equals to 1 observation of GDPR-related information regardless of Category or Area or Time-stamp.


Pie charts



The Overview-page also has the filtering option of selecting either / or only OneDrive, SharePoints or Emails (including Attachments).

Time Table

and a diachronic chart which displays the data of the accounts of the tenant.



Usage and Example

The three data-representations are interactive and intertwined and will respond to each other; a click on either one will shape the rest of the Overview-page.
Click around to filter through to the data you want to examine.

For example, a user may click on the green "GDPR"-portion in the Category-chart, then select "Work"; as their Domain and on the REPORT-symbol in the temporal chart to extract all Work-related GDPR-Cases related to that user in that period of time.

Any change, deletions or new mails, is reflected in the statistics following the a regular database update happening every 5. minute. Simply refresh the page or press the "Refresh and Reload" button.

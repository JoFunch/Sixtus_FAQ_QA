Once installed, the default setting of the scanner is not to scan anything, and users have to manually be added to the scanning-process.
If you do not see any data after being invited your account is either not added to the process properly or in the process of being scanned.


Yes! The Sixtus Scanner allows for deletion of emails of Accounts which are available to the user.

Simply hit the square to mark an Entry in the left column and press "Delete" and "Yes".
